Learn Sign Language

Sign language is a visual-manual communication system that utilizes hand gestures, facial expressions, and body movements to convey meaning, acting as a complete language with its own grammar and vocabulary, primarily used by people who are deaf or hard of hearing; importantly, different countries have distinct sign languages, meaning "sign language" is not universal, with American Sign Language (ASL) being the most widely used in the United States.

Common Sign Language Gestures

Hello - Wave your hand with the palm facing outward.

Thank You - Move your hand forward from your chin.

Please - Rub your open hand in a circular motion on your chest.
